Live charts, slicers and KPI visualizations — auto-updated from your data
• Slicers — the Period buttons (P1/P2/P3/All) and View dropdown filter every chart on this page at once.
• KPI cards — green ▲ means better than the previous period, red ▼ means worse; the small grey line explains the number in plain words.
• Trend & Debt charts — bars compare periods side by side; taller green/gold = stronger performance.
• Waterfall & Doughnut — the waterfall shows how revenue becomes profit step by step; the doughnut shows what the company owns.
• Radar & Gauges — bigger radar shape = healthier; each gauge needle in its green zone = the ratio beats its benchmark.
