HOW TO OPEN
-----------
1. FinAnalyser_Pro.html is a single self-contained file. No installation.
2. Double-click it (or right-click > Open With > your browser).
   Works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox. Keep an internet connection active the
   first time so the in-built libraries (Excel reader, charts, PDF) load.
3. Enter the access password when prompted to unlock the suite.
4. TIP: If a page ever looks blank or shows code as text, do a HARD REFRESH
   (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R). That clears the old cached copy.

SECTION 0  —  THE UNIVERSAL DATA FORMAT (read this first)
================================================================================
The software is "self-understanding": you give it a spreadsheet and it works
out the structure for you. You do NOT have to match fixed cells.

WHAT IT AUTO-DETECTS
  - The HEADER ROW: the first row that contains a label column named one of
    "Particulars", "Account", "Ledger Name", "Description", or "Financial Row".
  - The PERIOD COLUMNS: every column to the right of the label whose heading
    looks like a period — e.g. "Nov 2025", "FY 2024-25", "Current Year",
    "Previous Year", "Year-1", "Period 2". Add AS MANY as you like; the app
    reads them all and lets you compare/toggle between them.
  - The VALUES: the numbers under each period column.
  - SECTION HEADINGS: rows in CAPITALS with no number (e.g. "REVENUE",
    "EXPENSES", "ASSETS").
  - TOTAL/SUBTOTAL rows: rows whose label starts with "Total -" or contains
    words like "Total", "Gross Profit", "Net Profit/(Loss)".
  - ACCOUNT CODES (optional): a code like 3010000-01 in the label or in an
    "Account Code" column builds the parent/child hierarchy automatically.

THE TEMPLATE LAYOUT (download it from any panel: "Universal Template")
  Sheet "READ_ME"  -> plain-English instructions.
  Sheet "Data":
     Row 1:  FINANCIAL DATA TEMPLATE — FinAnalyser Pro
     Row 2:  COMPANY:          | Your Company Pvt Ltd
     Row 3:  STATEMENT TYPE:   | P&L   (or Balance Sheet / Trial Balance)
     Row 4:  UNIT:             | Actual  (or Thousands / Lakhs / Crores)
     Row 5:  (blank)
     Row 6:  Particulars | Account Code | Current Year | Previous Year | ...
     Row 7+: your data rows (add as many rows and period columns as needed)

RULES OF THUMB
  - Rows are dynamic: insert/delete as many as you need.
  - Columns are dynamic: add more period columns to the right — they appear
    automatically as extra comparison columns in the app.
  - Put income/positive figures as positive numbers; reductions as negative.
  - Keep the header words intact ("Particulars", period names) so detection
    stays reliable.
  - Supported uploads: .xlsx, .xls, .csv. You can also PASTE tab-separated
    data directly where a paste box is offered.

NOT SURE? Click "Load Sample Data" on any panel to see a complete, correct
example instantly, then replace it with your own numbers.

SECTION 10 —  FINANCIAL DRAFTING  (SCHEDULE III)   *** KEY MODULE ***
================================================================================
PURPOSE: Convert raw data into statutory Schedule III financial statements
         (Companies Act 2013) and export them to Excel and PDF.

TAB 1 — "FROM LOADED DATA"
   1. Load a file anywhere in the app (or click "Load Sample P&L" /
      "Load Sample Balance Sheet").
   2. Click "Render Schedule III from Loaded Data".
   3. The app maps your rows to the Schedule III Statement of Profit & Loss:
        I.  Revenue from Operations
        II. Other Income
        III.Total Revenue (I+II)
        IV. Expenses (Cost of Materials, Employee Benefits, Finance Costs,
            Depreciation & Amortisation, Other Expenses) -> Total Expenses
        V.  Profit/(Loss) before tax
        VI. Tax Expense
        VII.Profit/(Loss) for the period
      All loaded periods are shown side by side.

TAB 2 — "TRIAL BALANCE IMPORT"   (the full TB -> financials workflow)
   STEP 1: Upload a Trial Balance (.xlsx/.csv) — columns:
              Ledger Name | Account Code | Debit | Credit
           (or paste it, or click "Load Sample Trial Balance").
   STEP 2: Review Auto-Classification — every ledger is auto-mapped to a
           Schedule III head (Revenue, Employee Benefit, Finance Costs, Fixed
           Assets, Trade Receivables, Share Capital, Borrowings, Trade
           Payables, etc.). Override any mapping from the dropdown.
   STEP 3: Click "Generate Schedule III Financials" to produce a complete
           Balance Sheet (Equity & Liabilities / Assets) and Statement of P&L,
           with a tally check that Assets = Equity + Liabilities.

EXPORTS (top-right of the panel):
   - "Export Excel": a workbook with P&L, Balance Sheet, and Notes to Accounts.
   - "Export PDF":   a multi-page PDF (cover page + statements).

SHORTCUT: a green "Financial Drafting" button sits in the top toolbar for
          one-click access from anywhere.

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================
- "Page shows code as text / looks broken": hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) and
  make sure you opened the latest file.
- "No data on a page": load a file or click Load Sample Data first.
- "AI narrative didn't appear": the free AI service was busy — the Computed
  Analysis is still complete. Try again later.
- "Only one period detected": confirm your header row has clear period names
  (e.g. "Current Year", "FY 2024-25", "Nov 2025") in separate columns.
- "Figures look 100x off": check the UNIT row (Actual vs Lakhs vs Crores) and
  the Company Profile currency unit.
